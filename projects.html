System to manage legal work for lawyers in Sri Lanka

Independent lawyer or a partner in a firm, they have to manage lot of law cases each day and it is required to keep track of each law case for several years may be more than decade. Presently almost all the lawyers in Sri Lanka keep track of documents and ongoing case information manually without using a system. This project proposed a system so they can have following benefits:
• Improve quality service to customers
• Better workflow for faster delivery of legal assistance
• Reduced their firm’s operations cost
• Fexibility to work anywhere without carrying all the files
